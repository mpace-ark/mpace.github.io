Install Active Equipment
Your data room space relies upon direct and indirect cool air supplied to the front of your racks. Therefore, you must ensure that all in-rack blanking is fitted so that no gaps are present. When installing active equipment, you must also ensure that all devices are installed so that front-to-back cooling is maintained. If you need any support or guidance with blanking requirements, please contact BT Service Desk.
Cabling in and between Racks
Customers are free to use any fibre or copper cabling within their own rack. Any cabling between racks must utilise the overhead cable trays, and you must use cable compliant to EuroClass Cca–S1b –d1 –a2. Please note a Declaration of Performance “DOP” must be presented to the data centre when you are delivering and installing your cabling
